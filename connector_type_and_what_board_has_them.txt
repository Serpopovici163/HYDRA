Picoblade connectors (all connector types should only be used for one pinout to avoid issues from being able to connect the wrong things together)

2p --> V4 REC (SFTY SW)
3p --> V4 REC (CAM)
4p --> V4 LNK (AUX RADIO)
5p --> V4 LNK (AUX BATTERY)
7p --> V4 REC (PYRO)
8p --> V4 NAV (ACTUATION)
9p --> V4 PWR (CELL SENSE + EMAG + SHDN SW)
